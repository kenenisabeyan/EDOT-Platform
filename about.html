About EDOT Platform

EDOT (Education Digital Online Tutorials) was created with the goal of improving the quality of education for primary and secondary school students. We believe that every student deserves access to clear, supportive, and engaging learning resources.

Our platform offers structured online tutorials combined with guided in-person tutoring. This blended approach allows students to learn at their own pace while receiving personal academic support.

EDOT focuses on building strong academic foundations, boosting confidence, and preparing students for long-term educational success.
